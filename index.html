E L O N   M U S K

THE RICHEST MAN IN THE WORLD

Elon Musk is the charismatic co-founder of PayPal and Tesla, as well as the founder of SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company. He serves as CEO of Tesla and CEO/lead designer of SpaceX.

Born and raised in South Africa, Musk spent time in Canada before finally
moving to the U.S.

Educated at the University of Pennsylvania in physics, Musk started getting
his feet wet as a serial tech entrepreneur with early successes like Zip2 and X.com.

Musk has behaved in eccentric ways from time to time and has identified
himself as having Asperger's syndrome.
